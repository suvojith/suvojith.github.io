About Me

I'm a Forward Deployed Senior AI Engineer at EXL, building and shipping AI agents into production. I currently ship productionized agentic claims-processing systems for the insurance industry that autonomously analyze incidents, make decisions, and manage memory across interactions.

With 6.5+ years of experience across Generative AI, NLP, Computer Vision, Deep Learning, and traditional ML, I specialize in turning LLMs and diffusion models into reliable, production-ready applications following standard DevOps practices and CI/CD pipelines. I hold a Master's in AI/ML from BITS Pilani (NLP specialization) and lead a small team of AI engineers and data scientists, owning delivery end-to-end.

Previously at EY, Dunnhumby, and Infosys R&D, I've solved business problems across insurance, banking, retail, healthcare, legal, and hardware. I thrive in high-ambiguity environments where the path forward isn't yet defined.
